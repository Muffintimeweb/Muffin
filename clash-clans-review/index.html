Clash of Clans Review

by Yappo · August 7, 2017

Free-to-play has been in the air for some years now and is gradually spreading to all types of games. Games are so numerous on the Web and smartphones that to distinguish themselves, some adopt models offering to play for free in exchange for some optional purchases. Clash of Clans is no exception and since its launch in August 2012 it has been downloaded by hundreds of thousands of players and today has an impressive community. Full of qualities but also defects, is the game worth it as you try it in your turn?

THE STRATEGY GAME REVISITED

The mechanisms of Clash of Clans, you already know them if you are used to war strategy games such as the Age of Empire series. We start with the strict minimum of buildings and gradually we build others then we improve them before extending its city. Of course, in order for all this to be possible, resources are required and they are in the game of two: gold and elixir, accompanied by their own reservoirs. Then come the defenses, which are much more varied. There are of course the traditional ramparts but also cannons, towers of archers and mortars to attack from a distance, as well as traps like hidden bombs or even more zany, springs. By gradually improving all these facilities, Your city will become a real fortress and organize its buildings will quickly appear to you as a must. In addition to being practical (a simple drag and drop allows to change the place of a building), this system is paramount because you can not defend your base in real time with armies like in most games of the genre. So prepare for the best, because in your absence an enemy might well point out and plunder your resources.

Attack!

Obviously, on the other hand, you also have the ability to launch assaults. The creation of units is also simplified: one building to create one, another to improve, both can evolve to provide different and more powerful units. Once you have concocted your army, go into a battle against the goblins (single player mode) or directly in multi by selecting a player at random. The assault is quite complicated to conduct, it is actually just putting its units near the enemy and letting them get by. Certainly, depending on the type of warriors chosen, the targeted buildings will be different, but one can pester against this combat system which besides being particularly annoying, You forbid any control what necessarily implies that it will not happen exactly as you expected. In fact, once the units are laid, there is nothing left to do but to contemplate the battle in hopes of winning it. Positive point about attacks though, the ability to store units of allies of his clan that manifest themselves in the event of assault of your base.

SLOWNESS WHEN YOU HOLD US …

The existence of special heroes to unlock at very high level also energizes the battles. However, before you see the color of these heroes, you have time in front of you. Of course, Clash of Clan is not a completely free game and its developers have to feed themselves from time to time, so the presence of an in-game currency, called “gem” is not surprising. As in most free-to-play games of this kind, these gems are mainly used to save time, by finishing construction or unit formation. On the other hand, what is much more surprising are the completely abusive times required for basic actions. At first everything goes well, but it quickly degenerates. Take the gold mine for example, Building of which you will quickly have 6 copies. A mine takes only one minute to build, 5 minutes to evolve to level 2, then twelve hours from level 5 to 6 and five days from level 10 to level 11, which is absolutely huge! Of course, we say that it is to encourage in-game purchases but these are totally useless since once an improvement purchased, we always find ourselves having to wait without discovering new possibilities of gameplay. Purchases are also offered at exorbitant prices. To avoid waiting five days to improve the gold mine, it would be necessary to spend 750 gems or already more than 5 €. Multiply this figure by the number of buildings and evolutions, Add the necessary resources as well as the workforce (2 simultaneous constructions maximum, up to 5 while spending 25 € in the shop) and you will have a good idea of ​​the problem of this game: everything is extremely long and / or expensive . So you always have the possibility of fighting to wait, but because of having nothing else to do but to spend 5 minutes every other day, you will soon forget Clash of Clans and it’s a shame.

The notes
+Positive aspects
  • Beautifully done
  • The possibilities of defense of its base
  • A simple but not simplistic strategy game
-Negative points
  • Frustrating assaults
  • Abusive in-app purchases
  • A few minutes of play per day maximum, even when paying

Friendly to see and play, Clash of Clans is likely to attract you if you are used to social free-to-play on iOS or Facebook. Complete, but sufficiently accessible for the neophytes of the genre, the game proposes mechanics of strategy reviewed and simplified with a small tactical side very appreciable as regards the organization of the defenses of his village. However, the shortcomings of the attack phases and especially its incomprehensible economic system that makes the game extremely long, will certainly make you quickly let go of this Clash of Clans yet very promising.
