Invasion: Modern Empire

by Yappo · Published August 3, 2017 · Updated August 5, 2017

Invasion: Modern Empire is an exciting online warfare simulation and STR game type that challenges you to confront different enemy factions, conquer their territory and make their way to world domination in the midst of a global apocalypse . Fight to become the most powerful military commander in the world and walk towards victory!

Invasion Features: Modern Empire 

Your enemies are going to nail their nails with this STR type fighting style to ward off the apocalypse! 

• Military strategy, real – time fighting and building your base
• Destroy the world and rebuild it in its image by conquering enemy armies
• Strategic battles in 3D and real –

Action Fight for every inch of terrain and expand your Alliance territory! 

• Conquer enemy territory online in a world at war
• Claim territory by performing RAIDS on your enemies
• Conquer is yours, as long as you have soldiers and you organize raids on your enemies. But be careful – your enemies can also attack your base!

War tactics and advanced technology will help you move forward and get information about your enemies

• Modern warfare is how to spy on your enemies. Dominate your opponents by collecting information about their bases
• Command your troops and conquer territories using a panoramic map in real time! Fight against other alliances in the War of Monuments PVP!

Battle online against alliances and crush every alliance on your way

• Online multiplayer mode allows you to form an alliance and compete against other players to become the ultimate commander
• Online chat and other features will help you To find the perfect alliance
• Online multiplayer games feature PvP MMO combat on a modern battlefield
Fight with other alliances to occupy “war monuments”
• Raid monuments to experiment Changes and surprise real-time fights!
• Create diplomatic ties with other players as you see fit!
• PvP gameplay sends you to war alongside a ton of other players! Fight in the post-apocalyptic world of 2020!

Do you have what it takes to survive in this world at war?
